Youth Career Day 2.0

In order to fulfill its mission and support the development of young Tunisians, AIESEC in Tunisia- Monastir is organising a Youth Career Day (YCD) event. YCD is the link between startups, campanies and youth to exchange positions and CVs. Through YCD, young people can get a clear vision on their future career, they can learn more about their specialities and discover the Tunisian market.

We aim to give young people the chance to meet great people with great achievements, develop their potential, lead and impact. Don’t miss the chance to be part of one of the biggest events of AIESEC in Monasir in it’s 2st edition !
